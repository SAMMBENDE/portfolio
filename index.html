About me

Chasing

I listened to “my little inside voice”; and decided to give it a shot. I am happy my intuition got me here. While I enjoyed my previous role as a tech vendor for five years, the last year was so stressful that it prompted me to consider other career options. I gave myself a moment of break and looked into IT and its career possibilities. With a willingness to reach the goals I set for myself, I tapped inspiration from those with experience in the field who helped open my eyes to this side of the decor. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I adapted my schedule for a remote learning environment and earned two verifiable IT badges with IBM. Meanwhile, as a DJ, the thought of promoting my mixtapes and building an audience on the web made me develop a passion for programming. Curiosity gained control, so I started learning web development with the help of online resources myself, and the more I delved in, the more I realized there was so much to learn. I then took some in-depth training sessions on web development. Right now, I’m excited about new opportunities and putting my technical and soft skills into practice.
